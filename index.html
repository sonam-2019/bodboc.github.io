Tashi Group initiated CSR Project at Phuentsholing Town

As envisioned by His Majesty Jigme Khesar Namgyel Wangchuck, Tashi Group humbly takes this wonderful opportunity to uplift and recreate the greening of landscape of Phuentsholing town from main gate till... manidangrim. This humble and sincere gesture is dedicated to the commemoration of the 60th Birth Anniversary of our beloved Fourth Druk Gyalpo, His Majesty Jigme Singye Wangchuck. The CSR Project was inaugurated by His Excellency Lyonpo Dawa Gyaltshen, Minister of Home and Cultural Affairs, on November 8
